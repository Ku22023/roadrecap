This website is a multi-page website that helps a New Zealand teenager understand how to get their Learner Licence (and start preparing for Restricted!) we give lots of information!
In New Zealand, getting your learner licence is the first step to learning how to drive. You must be at least 16 years old and pass a theory test based on the NZ Road Code. To apply, you go to a licensing agent (like AA or VTNZ), bring acceptable ID, pass an eyesight check, have your photo taken, pay the fee, and sit the computer-based test. The test checks your knowledge of road rules, signs, and safe driving.
